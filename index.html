Welcome to our second server-wide contest.

Dates:

Start: July 1, 2017

Finish: July 31, 2017

Directions

  1. Create a permanent instant invite. Click here for directions on how to do this.
  2. Invite your friends to join the server using your permanent invite.
  3. If you invite the largest number of people to join the server, you win one prize of your choice from the prize pool!
    • This includes either a free Steam game key or a free ad in #announcements and an exclusive role.

Rules

  1. Please use only one permanent instant invite; otherwise, we cannot track your progress.
  2. If you use temporary invites, that progress is lost. We only count from your permanent invite. This does not reset your progress, but any invite from the temporary one is nullified from the contest.
  3. If you invite someone who causes issues and/or is kicked/banned, they will not count as an invite.
  4. If you invite 3 or more people who create problems, you may be elminated from the contest at the moderators' discretion.
  5. Users who quickly leave or leave before making conversation are not counted.
    • We do not subtract a point if you invite someone who makes an effort and discovers the server is not meant for them.
    • "Making an effort" is determined at the staff's discretion, but know that leaving within 24 hours after sending only one message is likely not "making an effort."
