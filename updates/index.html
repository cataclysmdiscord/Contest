Welcome to the Contest!

Post by Hope on July 1, 2017.

Welcome to the Cataclysm Discord's contest updates! Every week we'll be posting who's in the lead here, along with any other necessary updates. Please make sure to check out the home page to view the contest rules, directions, and active dates. If you have any questions, just ask a @Staff member.

We guarantee that the winner may choose a prize from one of three games. There is a chance that the amount of available games may increase, thus allowing the winner to choose from one of four or five games.

So far, we've decided on two of the potential prizes to be placed into the "prize pool." They include Particula and Revolution Ace. Don't fret if you're not interested in either of these games! The other prize option(s) will be revealed on Friday, July 7, 2017.
